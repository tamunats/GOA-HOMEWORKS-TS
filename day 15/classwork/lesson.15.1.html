Van Gogh's family in his art

Van Gogh's family in his art refers to works that Vincent van Gogh made for or about Van Gogh family members. In 1881, Vincent drew a portrait of his grandfather, also named Vincent van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh (grandfather)

Vincent van Gogh's grandfather (born 1789) was also named Vincent van Gogh

Father

Theodorus van Gogh was born 8 February 1822, one of eleven children and the only one of six brothers to become a pastor like his father.
